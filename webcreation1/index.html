About us

A pioneer in choice of locations and style. Our gallery was established in a soho style space, unique in Montreal for its time. With over 3000 sq. feet, our goal was to open in a space that could display emerging and top tier Canadian artists.

Our services

We offer a wide variety of services to artists and art lovers in the community.

Art evaluation and advising

Conducting formal appraisals of artworks, as well as offering personalized guidance to collectors on building their art collections, including advice on acquisitions, valuations, and investment strategies.

Consignment

We are actively seeking works by the Canadian artists for purchase or consignment. Consignment for a period of 6 to 12 months. No hidden fees for insurance, framing, photography, or storage.

Art framing and installation

Providing custom framing services and professional installation for artworks, ensuring they are displayed safely and aesthetically.

Art conservation and restoration

Partnering with conservators and restoration specialists to preserve and restore artworks, ensuring their longevity and maintaining their aesthetic integrity.
